About

Hi! My name is Molly Murtaugh. I am web and graphic designer, currently living in sunny San Diego! I have a passion for helping others conceptualize their companies and create digital and print branding that brings that idea to life. While I do have a distinct style, I welcome all collaborations with existing ideas and branding you may have. When I am not working on design, I spend my time teaching yoga and exploring the West with my pup, Howard. Shoot me an email and tell me a bit about yourself and how you found me. I would love to hear from you!
