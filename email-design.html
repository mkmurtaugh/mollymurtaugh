Email Design



Project Details | Design Responsive Marketing Emails

Client | Pura Vida Bracelets

Creative Fields | Graphic Design {layout design + typography + illustration}

Programs+ Mediums | Photoshop + Illustrator

Summary + Results | You have a very small window to engage your customer via email marketing. Often emails are clicked on just to get them to mark as unread or even clicked through to delete. Creating an email template that is bright and catchy, yet simple enough to deliver the intended message is not always easy. In this particular email for our Retro Femme launch, the goal was to create one section that is bright and vibey, featuring the product by drawing the consumers eye. Using the retro swoop custom asset as well as the light leaks and general color scheme carried through I was able to effectively draw the users attention and drive traffic to the website for this launch.
